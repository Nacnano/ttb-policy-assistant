# Data Classification Policy

## Overview

TTB Bank classifies all data assets into four tiers to ensure appropriate handling, storage, and protection. All employees who handle bank data must understand and apply these classifications.

## Classification Tiers

### Public

Data that is approved for public release or is already publicly available.

**Examples**: Press releases, published annual reports, product brochures.

**Handling**: No special restrictions. May be shared freely.

### Internal

Data intended for internal use only, not for distribution outside the bank.

**Examples**: Internal memos, operational procedures, staff directories.

**Handling**: Must not be shared externally without approval. Store on bank-approved systems.

### Confidential

Sensitive business or customer data that could cause significant harm if disclosed.

**Examples**: Customer account data, loan applications, employee salary information, financial forecasts.

**Handling**: Access on a need-to-know basis only. Must be encrypted in transit. Shared only via approved secure channels.

### Restricted

Highly sensitive data subject to regulatory obligations or with severe harm potential if disclosed.

**Examples**: Board-level strategy documents, regulatory examination reports, PEP customer data, AML investigation files.

**Handling**: **Encryption required** for storage and transit. Access restricted to named individuals only. Physical documents must be locked in secure cabinets. Digital copies must be watermarked.

## Handling Rules Summary

| Classification | Email | USB | Cloud | Encryption Required |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Public | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | No |
| Internal | ✓ (internal only) | IT-approved only | Bank cloud only | No |
| Confidential | Encrypted email only | Prohibited | Bank cloud + encrypted | In transit |
| Restricted | Prohibited | Prohibited | Prohibited | Always |

## Data Labelling

All documents and files must be labelled with their classification tier in the header or filename where practicable.

## Data Breach

Any unauthorised disclosure of Confidential or Restricted data must be reported to the CISO and Compliance within 1 hour of discovery.
