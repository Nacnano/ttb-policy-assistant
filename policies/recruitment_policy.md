# Recruitment and Onboarding Policy

## Overview

This policy covers the end-to-end recruitment process at TTB Bank, from vacancy approval through to employee onboarding. It applies to all hiring managers and HR personnel.

## Vacancy Approval

Before recruiting, the hiring manager must obtain approval from:

1. Department Head
2. HR Business Partner
3. Finance (for budget confirmation)

## Job Posting

All vacancies must be posted internally for at least **5 working days** before external advertising. Internal candidates who meet the minimum requirements must be interviewed.

## Selection Process

The standard selection process includes:

1. CV screening by HR
2. Competency-based interview with hiring manager
3. Technical assessment (where applicable)
4. Final interview with Department Head or above
5. Background check and reference checks

## Background Checks

A **criminal background check** is mandatory for all new hires before an offer letter is issued. Roles with access to financial systems or customer data also require a credit check.

## Probation Period

All new employees undergo a **3-month probation period**. Performance is formally reviewed at the end of month 2. The probation period may be extended by up to 3 months with documented justification.

## Referral Bonus

Employees who refer a successful candidate who completes 3 months of employment are eligible for a **referral bonus of ฿15,000**, paid with the next payroll cycle.

## Onboarding

All new employees must complete:

- Bank induction programme (2 days, delivered by HR)
- Compliance and AML training (online, mandatory within first week)
- IT security awareness training (online, mandatory within first week)
- Role-specific training as defined by the hiring manager

## Offer Letters

Offer letters must be issued by HR only and must contain the approved compensation package. Verbal offers without a formal offer letter are not binding.
