# Business Travel Policy

## Overview

This policy covers domestic and international travel undertaken on behalf of TTB Bank. All business travel must be pre-approved by the employee's manager and booked through the bank's approved travel management system.

## Air Travel

| Flight Duration | Permitted Class |
|---|---|
| Under 4 hours | Economy only |
| 4–8 hours | Economy (Business class requires VP approval) |
| 8 hours or more | Business class permitted |

Employees must book the lowest available fare that meets the class criteria. Personal loyalty programme upgrades are permitted but must not result in additional cost to the bank.

## Hotel Accommodation

Standard hotel rate caps:

| Location | Maximum Rate |
|---|---|
| Bangkok | ฿4,000 per night |
| Major Thai cities (Chiang Mai, Phuket, etc.) | ฿3,000 per night |
| International destinations | As approved by Finance |

Hotels must be booked through the travel management system. Exceptions require Finance approval.

## Per Diem

Daily per diem rates for domestic travel (covering meals and incidentals):

| Destination | Per Diem |
|---|---|
| Bangkok | ฿600 per day |
| Other Thai provinces | ฿500 per day |
| International | As per Finance rate table |

## Ground Transportation

- Taxis and ride-sharing (Grab) are reimbursable for business purposes.
- Employees should use public transport where practical.
- Rental cars require prior approval.

## Travel Advances

Employees may request a travel advance of up to 80% of estimated expenses 5 days before travel. Advances must be reconciled within 7 days of return.

## Approval Process

1. Submit travel request in HR/Finance portal.
2. Manager approval required.
3. Finance approval required for international travel or trips exceeding ฿50,000.
4. Book via approved travel system after approval.
