# Leave Policy

## Overview

This policy governs all types of employee leave at TMBThanachart Bank (TTB). It applies to all permanent employees unless otherwise stated.

## Annual Leave

Employees are entitled to annual leave based on years of service:

- **Less than 5 years of service**: 15 days per year
- **5 years or more of service**: 20 days per year

Annual leave must be approved by the employee's direct manager at least 5 working days in advance. Unused annual leave may be carried over for up to 12 months. Any carry-over balance not used within 12 months will be forfeited.

## Sick Leave

All employees are entitled to **30 days** of paid sick leave per calendar year. A medical certificate from a licensed physician is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry over to the following year.

## Maternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to **98 days** of maternity leave (approximately 14 weeks), which includes both pre- and post-natal leave. The first 45 days are paid at full salary; the remaining 53 days are paid at 50% salary as per labour law requirements.

## Paternity Leave

Male employees are entitled to **5 days** of paid paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.

## Personal Leave

Employees may take up to **3 days** of personal leave per year for urgent personal matters. Personal leave requires manager approval and is not convertible to pay.

## Public Holidays

Employees receive all official Thai public holidays as declared by the Thai government each year, typically around **13–15 days**.

## Leave Request Procedure

1. Submit a leave request via the HR portal at least 5 working days in advance (except for emergency leave).
2. Manager must approve or reject within 2 working days.
3. HR records are updated upon manager approval.

## Contact

For leave-related queries, contact HR Operations at hr@ttb.co.th.
