# Remote Work Policy

## Overview

TTB Bank supports flexible working arrangements through a hybrid work model. This policy governs the conditions under which employees may work from home or other remote locations.

## Eligibility

Employees who have completed their probation period and whose roles are deemed eligible by their department head may participate in remote work arrangements.

## Work-From-Home Days

Employees are permitted a **maximum of 2 work-from-home (WFH) days per week**, subject to operational requirements and manager approval. Core banking operations, front-line branch staff, and roles requiring physical presence are exempt.

## VPN Requirement

**VPN use is mandatory** when accessing any bank system remotely. Employees must connect to the bank VPN before accessing internal applications, email, or data.

## Prohibited Locations

Employees must not work from:

- **Public Wi-Fi networks** (cafes, airports, hotels) without VPN
- Locations outside Thailand for more than 14 consecutive days without HR and Compliance approval
- Locations that do not have a reasonably private and secure workspace

## Equipment

The bank provides approved laptops for remote work. Employees must not use personal computers to access bank systems unless formally enrolled in the BYOD programme. Bank laptops must not be used by family members.

## Physical Security

- The screen must not be visible to other household members when handling confidential data
- Printed bank documents must be stored securely and shredded when no longer needed
- Lost or stolen bank devices must be reported to IT Security within 1 hour

## Availability

Remote employees are expected to maintain the same working hours as if in the office and be reachable via bank-approved communication channels (Microsoft Teams, email) during core hours (9:00–17:00 BKT).

## Performance

Remote work is a privilege, not a right. Poor performance or policy non-compliance may result in the arrangement being withdrawn.
