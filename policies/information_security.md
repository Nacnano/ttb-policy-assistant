# Information Security Policy

## Overview

This policy establishes TTB Bank's information security requirements to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of bank information assets. All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to bank systems must comply.

## Password Requirements

All user accounts must comply with the following password standards:

- **Minimum length: 12 characters**
- Must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character
- Passwords must not contain the user's name, username, or common words
- Passwords must be changed every **90 days**
- Passwords must not be reused for the previous 12 cycles
- Passwords must never be written down or shared

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

**MFA is mandatory** for all systems that support it, including:

- Email and collaboration tools
- VPN access
- Banking core systems
- Cloud management consoles
- HR and Finance portals

## Access Control

- Access to systems must be granted on a **least-privilege, need-to-know basis**
- All access requests must be approved by the system owner and IT Security
- Access must be reviewed every 6 months and revoked immediately upon role change or termination
- Shared accounts are prohibited; each user must have a unique login

## Security Incident Reporting

All suspected or confirmed security incidents must be reported to the IT Security team **within 1 hour** of discovery. This includes:

- Suspected malware or ransomware
- Unauthorised access to systems or data
- Lost or stolen devices containing bank data
- Phishing emails (forward to phishing@ttb.co.th)

## Device Security

- All bank-issued devices must have full-disk encryption enabled
- Automatic screen lock must be set to no more than 5 minutes of inactivity
- Personal devices must not store bank data unless enrolled in the Mobile Device Management (MDM) system

## Patch Management

All systems must be patched within:

- **Critical patches**: within 24 hours of release
- **High patches**: within 7 days
- **Medium/Low patches**: within 30 days
