# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Overview

This policy outlines the rules for claiming and approving business-related expenses at TTB Bank. All employees must comply with this policy when incurring expenses on behalf of the bank.

## Meal Allowance

Employees on approved business travel or working beyond standard hours are entitled to a meal allowance of **฿600 per day**. Receipts are required for claims above ฿500. Alcohol-related expenses are **not reimbursable** under any circumstances.

## Receipts and Documentation

- All expense claims must be supported by original receipts or valid tax invoices.
- Receipts for amounts above **฿500** are mandatory.
- Electronic receipts (PDF, email confirmations) are accepted.
- Lost receipts require a signed declaration of loss and manager countersignature.

## Approval Timeline

- Expense claims must be submitted within **7 calendar days** of incurring the expense.
- Late submissions require VP-level approval.
- Managers must approve or reject claims within 3 working days.

## Prohibited Expenses

The following expenses will not be reimbursed:

- Alcoholic beverages
- Personal entertainment (movies, personal travel, etc.)
- Fines, penalties, or parking tickets
- Gifts without prior approval (see Code of Conduct Policy)
- First-class travel (unless approved by C-suite)

## Entertainment Expenses

Client entertainment expenses require pre-approval from the department head and must include the business purpose and names of all attendees. Per-head cost for client dinners must not exceed **฿2,000 per person**.

## Mileage Claims

Employees using personal vehicles for bank business may claim mileage at the rate of **฿4 per kilometre**. A mileage log (destination, purpose, km) must accompany all mileage claims.

## Submission Process

1. Log expenses in the Finance portal (finance.ttb.internal).
2. Attach scanned receipts.
3. Submit for manager approval.
4. Finance processes approved claims within 5 working days.
